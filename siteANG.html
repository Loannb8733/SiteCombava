Presentation

Combava and David's Lemonade... what is it?


We are an association of organic citrus fruit producers, we are French and independent.
We would like to offer you private events all over the country to let you taste our lemonades.


We're offering you a snack, and we already know you'll like it.


Through these 6 meetings, you will not only be able to taste, but you can also choose to support an organic production of fresh and quality products by leaving with your lemonade.


Seeing is good, coming is even better!


Our meetings take place in 6 cities in France on 6 different dates in which you will have the opportunity to share with our producers, local associations, other shops and much more!


Come, yes, but how?


Nothing could be simpler, all you have to do is fill in the registration form to go to one or more of the events we offer, and meet us there!
